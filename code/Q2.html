How does driver's experience impact performance?

Processing of data

The data for this visualisation was processed using Python Pandas dataframes. The datasets used were the “drivers”, “driver_standings”, and “races” from the Formula 1 World Championship (1950 - 2023) dataset on Kaggle. Firstly, null values were checked to ensure that the data was ready for processing. The number of seasons each driver has competed in Formula 1 was then calculated to determine their years of experience. Next, the average points earned per season by each driver was calculated to associate it with their years of experience and compute the average points earned per season of experience in general. This approach helps identify trends in whether a driver's experience has an impact on their performance. The resulting data from this processing was exported as a CSV file that the visualisation is using to present the data.
